# H4 Conversion Copywriting Skill

## Purpose
Create clear, modern, conversion-focused messaging for H4 Systems client projects.

This skill improves:
- clarity
- trust
- professionalism
- lead conversion
- positioning
- customer understanding

The goal is not to sound "salesy."

The goal is to:
- communicate value quickly
- reduce confusion
- increase trust
- encourage action

---

# Primary Responsibilities

This skill handles:
- Hero sections
- Headlines
- Subheadlines
- Calls-to-action (CTAs)
- Service descriptions
- Homepage messaging
- Landing page copy
- Value propositions
- Website section rewrites
- Brand positioning
- Trust messaging

---

# H4 Messaging Philosophy

Messaging should feel:
- Modern
- Professional
- Clean
- Confident
- Practical
- Business-focused

Avoid:
- hype language
- buzzword overload
- robotic AI wording
- generic agency language
- overly corporate tone
- "synergy" style phrasing

Avoid phrases like:
- "cutting-edge solutions"
- "revolutionary platform"
- "best-in-class"
- "next-generation innovation"
- "we help businesses succeed"

Prefer:
- specific outcomes
- operational improvements
- clarity
- trust
- simplicity

---

# Writing Style Rules

## Always:
- Write clearly
- Keep sentences concise
- Use modern business language
- Focus on customer outcomes
- Prioritize readability
- Sound experienced but approachable
- Emphasize business value over technical jargon

## Prefer:
- shorter paragraphs
- strong headline structure
- active voice
- clean formatting
- straightforward language

---

# CTA Rules

CTAs should feel:
- confident
- modern
- friction-light

Avoid weak CTAs like:
- Learn More
- Submit
- Click Here

Prefer:
- Schedule a Consultation
- Start Your Modernization
- Get a Website Audit
- See What's Possible
- Modernize Your Business
- Build Smarter Operations

---

# Headline Rules

Headlines should:
- communicate value quickly
- avoid fluff
- avoid excessive length
- focus on outcomes

Good examples:
- Modern Websites Built for Business Growth
- Smarter Digital Systems for Modern Businesses
- Modernize Your Website and Operations
- Websites, Dashboards, and Automation Designed to Scale
- Business-First Digital Solutions Built for Growth

Avoid:
- vague messaging
- overly creative wording
- unclear metaphors
- technical buzzwords

---

# Subheadline Rules

Subheadlines should:
- expand on the headline
- clarify services
- explain outcomes
- improve trust

Good example:
"We deliver modern websites, dashboards, and automation designed to launch faster, scale smarter, and create better customer experiences."

---

# Value Proposition Rules

Focus on:
- speed

# SEO Guidance

When appropriate:
- naturally include service keywords
- prioritize readability over keyword stuffing

---

# Rewrite Strategy

When rewriting:
1. Simplify confusing wording
2. Remove unnecessary filler
3. Improve clarity
4. Improve trust
5. Improve flow
6. Improve CTA strength
7. Modernize tone

Do not completely change the meaning unless requested.

---

# Output Structure

When rewriting sections, always show:
1. **Original** — quoted exactly
2. **Rewrite** — the new version
3. **Why** — one sentence explaining the change

When writing from scratch, deliver:
- Headline
- Subheadline
- CTA recommendations
- Optionally explain reasoning briefly

---

# Example Transformation

Before:
"We provide innovative digital solutions for businesses."

After:
"Modern websites, dashboards, and automation designed to help your business operate smarter and scale faster."

---

# Final Rule

Good copy should make the business:
- easier to understand
